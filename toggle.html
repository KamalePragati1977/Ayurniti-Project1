Kapha

Kapha is based on Earth and Water. It can be described as steady, stable, heavy, and grounded.

Kapha governs structure, stability, and lubrication within the body. People with a Kapha constitution often have a calm temperament, well-built bodies, and smooth, radiant skin.

  • Kapha governs growth and repair.
  • It is linked to cold and wet environments.
  • Kapha individuals thrive with warm, dry climates.
